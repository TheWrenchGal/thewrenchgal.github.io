About Andlang Photography and the Blog

Andlang Photography is based on the idea that there is beauty to be found in everything and capturing that beauty can help bring joy to others.

The blog on the other hand…well, that’s more of a dumpster of random misadventures. You’ll find a mixture of trip stories, projects, and what did and did not go well along the way.
